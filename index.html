A Day in the Life of a Computer Science Student

"My day as a Computer Science student at UNISEL starts with a dose of coffee and a quick review of my to-do list. Mornings are usually packed with lectures—algorithms, AI, and software development classes. After lunch, I head to the library to work on my Final Year Project, brainstorming ideas for my AI-powered career recommendation system. Evenings are for unwinding: a game of futsal with friends or a round of chess online. Balancing academics and hobbies isn’t always easy, but consistency keeps me on track."

The Power of Consistency: My Motivation Journey

"Consistency has been my secret weapon in everything I do. Whether it’s coding every day, improving my chess skills, or staying active with football, small, consistent efforts have always led to big results. One example is my coding journey: starting with 30 minutes a day, I built the foundation for more advanced projects over time. Consistency isn’t just about discipline—it’s about believing in the process and trusting that progress will follow."

Why I Love Drawing: A Creative Escape from the Digital World

"Amidst all the screens and code, drawing offers me a refreshing escape into the analog world. Whether it’s sketching a football scene or experimenting with abstract designs, drawing allows me to express ideas that words or code can’t. One of my favorite projects was sketching a futuristic city—it started as a random doodle during a lecture but turned into a full-blown artwork that now hangs on my wall."
